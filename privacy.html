Privacy Policy

How Droidunclock collects, uses, stores, and protects customer information for repairs, orders, payments, and support.

Last updated: April 29, 2026

Information We Collect

We may collect your name, email address, phone number, shipping or billing address, device model, repair notes, order details, payment status, support messages, and website usage information needed to provide our services.

How We Use Information

We use customer information to respond to repair requests, provide quotes, process shop orders, arrange delivery or pickup, send service updates, prevent fraud, maintain records, improve the website, and comply with applicable obligations.

Payments

Card and PayPal payments are handled by third-party payment providers. Droidunclock does not store full card details on this website.

Sharing Information

We may share limited information with service providers that help operate the website, process payments, manage orders, deliver items, provide email or chat support, host data, or comply with legal requests.

Cookies and Analytics

The website may use essential cookies, local storage, or similar technologies to support cart, checkout, authentication, language preferences, security, and website performance.

Data Retention

We keep information only as long as reasonably needed for service delivery, support, accounting, security, warranty handling, dispute resolution, and legal compliance.

Your Choices

You can request access, correction, deletion, or restriction of your personal information by contacting droidunclock@gmail.com. Some information may need to be retained where required for legal, tax, security, or transaction records.

Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect customer information. No online service can guarantee absolute security, so customers should avoid sending unnecessary sensitive information through forms or chat.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent to droidunclock@gmail.com.
